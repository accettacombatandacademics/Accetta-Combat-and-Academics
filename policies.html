Policies

Cancellations & rescheduling

Your session time is reserved just for you. The notice periods below help cover the preparation and time set aside for each lesson, and let me offer freed-up slots to other students who would otherwise have booked them. They apply to both tutoring and boxing.

More than 24 hours notice
No charge

Cancel or reschedule for free, no questions asked.

Between 12 and 24 hours
50% fee

50% of the session fee applies to cancellations or reschedules in this window.

Within 12 hours
100% fee

The full session fee applies, including no-shows.

I understand unexpected circumstances can arise, and I will always do my best to accommodate where possible and find another time that works. To cancel or reschedule, just message me as early as you can. Thank you for your understanding — and please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions.

Additional charges. In-person tutoring across Melbourne is available for an additional charge; online sessions have no travel fee. Boxing sessions are held at the gym, and training at another location can be arranged for an additional charge.

Questions about a booking?

If you need to change a session or have any questions, just reach out.
